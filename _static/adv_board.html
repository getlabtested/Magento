Gonorrhea is one of the most common STD tests we conduct. Its a simple urine test, and uses a process called Nucleic acid amplification to look for the genetic material or DNA of Gonorrhea bacteria. It is the most accurate type of Gonorrhea test available.

Your lab experience should be a painless and pleasant one. No one will know you're being tested for Gonorrhea because the lab conducts hundreds of different types of tests. You shouldn't have to wait more than a few minutes, and the test itself simply involves peeing in a cup. You wont have to get undressed, there won't be any examination, and you should be out the door in 15 minutes.

Just remember to avoid urinating for 2 hours before taking your test. Its also important to remember that other sexually transmitted diseases may be present at the same time as Gonorrhea, so people often will get tested for all STDs at the same time. Taking them at once will save considerable money and give you added peace of mind.
